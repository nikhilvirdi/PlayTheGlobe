# PlayTheGlobe

The toughest geography guessing game on the internet. Every political entity on Earth — UN members, microstates, territories, unrecognized states, dependencies, all 250+ of them. No difficulty settings. No handholding. Full pool, always.

## What is this

PlayTheGlobe is a retro pixel-art geography game built for people who think they know the world. Two core modes, fourteen+ game modes total, AI-generated hints that never repeat, a global leaderboard, live duels, seasonal rankings, and a country collection map that will take you months to complete.

## Game Modes

**Core**
- Flag Mode — progressive flag reveal, 6 attempts
- Globe Mode — hot/cold distance feedback on a 3D interactive globe
- Reverse Mode — place a pin on a blank globe where you think the country is, scored by km accuracy
- Hard Mode — Globe mode with zero feedback, just right or wrong

**Speed**
- Blitz Speedrun — how many countries or flags can you correctly identify in 60 seconds
- Trivia Blitz — rapid fire geography trivia, points based

**Binary Comparison**
- Population Poker — which country has higher population, streak based
- Area Showdown — which country has larger land area, streak based
- Oldest/Youngest Nation — which country gained independence first, streak based
- GDP Ladder — rank 4 countries by GDP, drag and drop

**Knowledge**
- Capital Chaos — given a capital city, guess the country
- Currency Match — given a currency name or symbol, guess the country
- Language Roots — given a paragraph in an unknown language, identify the country
- Timeline Nation — given a historical event, guess the country
- Who's From Where — given a cryptic clue about a famous person, guess their birth country
- Anthem Drop — first 10 seconds of a national anthem, guess the country

**Visual**
- Shape Shifter — pure country outline on a blank background, no context
- Coat of Arms — identify the country from its coat of arms
- Flag Builder — reassemble scrambled flag elements via drag and drop

**Chain / Endurance**
- Border Chain — build the longest possible chain of border-adjacent countries
- Elimination Mode — 3 lives per session, exhaust them and your score gets submitted

**Competitive**
- Head to Head Live Duels — same country, race to guess it first, PvP leaderboard
- Daily Gauntlet — 5 countries back to back, win it for bonus points, resets every 24 hours

## Hint System

Every round gives you 4 hint slots. Hints are AI-generated by Gemini Pro, calibrated to the country's global fame level. Famous countries never get capital city giveaways — even the 50% hint for USA will be something like "the country that purchased Alaska from Russia." Obscure countries get proportionally more revealing hints because just knowing they exist is half the battle.

- Slot 1 — costs 10% of current points, maximally vague
- Slot 2 — costs 15% of current points, slightly less vague
- Slot 3 — costs 35% of current points, narrows it down significantly
- Slot 4 — costs 50% of current points, near-giveaway but never obvious

Hints draw from historical events, cultural references, language families, cuisine, geopolitics, terrain, rivers, mountains, neighbouring states, famous persons born there, and anything else in the dataset. No two playthroughs generate the same hints.

## Progression

- **Global Leaderboard** — ranked by a formula combining streak, points, and countries collected. The player with the most countries and most points sits at the top.
- **Ranked Seasons** — monthly resets, all-time records preserved. Season titles: Bronze Globe, Silver Atlas, Gold Cartographer, Platinum Explorer, Diamond Geographer.
- **Country Collection Map** — a personal world map that fills in every country you correctly guess. All 250+ to collect. Country mastery tiers per country: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum.
- **Continent Badges** — collect every country in a continent to earn its badge. All 6 badges unlock the Globe Master title.
- **Streak Flame** — your streak counter has a flame that grows with your streak. At 50+ it starts consuming the screen edges.
- **Milestone Rewards** — Welcome to Earth, Nauru? Really?, Century Club, Cartographer God, and more.

## Competitive Features

- **Head to Head Live Duels** — real-time, same country, first correct guess wins
- **Daily Gauntlet** — 5 countries, everyone gets the same sequence that day, win for bonus points
- **Friend Rooms** — private room with a 6-digit code, persistent private leaderboard for your group
- **Challenge a Player** — async duel from the global leaderboard, notified on next login
- **Taunt System** — one-tap pre-written taunts sent after beating someone
- **Nearby Rivals** — always see the two players ranked just above and below you
- **Live Globe Activity Feed** — real-time ticker of what players worldwide are doing right now

## Dataset Sources

- REST Countries API — capitals, population, area, borders, currencies, languages
- flagcdn.com — flag images, all countries, free CDN
- Wikimedia Commons — coat of arms images, public domain
- Natural Earth — GeoJSON country outlines for Shape Shifter and collection map
- World Bank API — GDP data per country
- Wikidata SPARQL — famous persons by birth country, historical events, UNESCO sites
- National anthem audio — static dataset, all public domain


## Account System

Username and password. Globally unique usernames, first come first served. Cross-device via backend persistence. No OAuth, no third party auth, no email required.


## How Scoring Works

Each round starts with a base point value. Every hint slot used deducts a percentage of your current points for that round. Your global leaderboard score is a formula across three dimensions — streak length, average points per round, and total countries collected. Winning the Daily Gauntlet adds a flat bonus to your global score. Seasonal leaderboards reset monthly but your all-time records never reset.
